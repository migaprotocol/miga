\section{The Cyrus Legacy: From Empire to Exile}
% =============================================================================

\subsection{The First Declaration of Human Rights (539 BCE)}

Twenty-five centuries ago, Cyrus the Great conquered Babylon not with destruction, but with liberation. The Cyrus Cylinder---now housed in the British Museum---records the first declaration of human rights in human history:

\begin{quote}
\textit{``I freed the people from their chains. I restored their temples. I returned their gods. I gathered all their peoples and restored them to their homelands.''}
\end{quote}

This was not mere propaganda. Cyrus established an empire built on \textbf{pluralism}---many peoples, many faiths, one system of law. Satrapies governed themselves within bounds. Diversity was structural, not merely tolerated.

\subsection{From Cyrus to the Present: The Long Arc}

\begin{longtable}{p{3cm}p{4cm}p{6cm}}
\toprule

\subsection{The Constitutional Revolution (1905--1911)}

Iran's Constitutional Revolution was the first of its kind in Asia---a movement for representative government, rule of law, and limits on arbitrary power. Though ultimately crushed, it planted seeds that still grow.

The revolutionaries understood: \textbf{power must be constrained by structure, not just goodwill}.

\subsection{1953: The Coup That Changed Everything}

Operation Ajax. The CIA and MI6 overthrew Prime Minister Mohammad Mosaddegh---Iran's democratically elected leader---to restore the Shah and protect oil interests.

The consequences echo still:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Trust in Western democracy: shattered
    \item Path to 1979 revolution: opened
    \item 8+ million Iranians now in diaspora
    \item A civilization scattered across 85+ countries
\end{itemize}

\subsection{The Diaspora: A Nation Without Borders}

Today, the Persian diaspora represents:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{8+ million} people across 40+ countries
    \item \textbf{Highly skilled}: engineers, doctors, entrepreneurs, artists
    \item \textbf{Capital-connected}: significant economic influence
    \item \textbf{Culturally cohesive}: shared language, history, identity
    \item \textbf{Yet fragmented}: no coordination infrastructure
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Bonding Curve Mechanics}

The bonding curve provides fair price discovery:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Start Price}: 0.0000001 SOL per token
    \item \textbf{End Price}: 0.00001 SOL per token (100x increase)
    \item \textbf{Curve}: Linear progression based on tokens sold
    \item \textbf{Slippage Protection}: Minimum tokens out parameter
\end{itemize}

\begin{equation}
\text{Price} = \text{StartPrice} + \frac{\text{TokensSold}}{\text{TotalSupply}} \times (\text{EndPrice} - \text{StartPrice})
\end{equation}
