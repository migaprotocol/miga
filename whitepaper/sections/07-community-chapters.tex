\section{Community Chapters}
% =============================================================================

A Chapter is a gauge. It represents a community---physical or virtual---that serves the diaspora.

\subsection{Chapter Types}

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{City Chapters}: Los Angeles, Toronto, London, Berlin, Sydney, Dubai
    \item \textbf{University Chapters}: Stanford, MIT, Oxford, Toronto, TUM
    \item \textbf{Guild Chapters}: Developers, Artists, Researchers, Entrepreneurs
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Launching a Chapter}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Gather 10 founding members with vePARS
    \item Submit proposal to governance
    \item Pass with 51\% approval
    \item Receive initial budget, begin operations
\end{enumerate}

Chapters earn PARS emissions proportional to their votes. They report quarterly.
